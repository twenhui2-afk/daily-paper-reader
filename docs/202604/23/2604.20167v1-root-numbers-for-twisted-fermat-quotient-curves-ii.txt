The study of the root numbers of abelian varieties over number fields is one of the main streams in arithmetic geometry. Especially, CM abelian varieties are attractive and accessible examples to carry out the computation (see [8, Section 1]). This paper is a sequel of our previous work [9] and we continue to study the root numbers of the CM abelian varieties which are quotients of the Jacobian varieties of the twisted Fermat curves.
